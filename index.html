Other Projects

As part of an inclusive peace movement, I am a practicing vegan. This philosophy, lifestyle, and identity affects many aspects of one's life, challenging cultural norms, biological and environmental science, and psycho-social identities and roles.

In order to better understand these aspects, I host an almost-monthly discussion group called Becoming Vegan at various locations around Columbus and on Facebook at the Becoming Vegan Facebook Group. More information, including current plans and past monthly readings, can be found at the Becoming Vegan website:

http://becoming-vegan.info

My fianceé and I also blog about our pet-owning and fostering experiences at The Jaeger Fund Blog, accessible at the following address:

http://TheJaegerFund.com

Vegan dogs, in particular, are an interest of ours, as we believe that vegan dogs can be healthy and happy, while contributing to the inclusive peace movement in the same ways as do vegan humans. We are blogging about our experiences at the Vegan Dog Blog, accessible:
